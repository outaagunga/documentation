


## 1. ORDERLY ROOM PROCEEDINGS CHECK  

Purpose: Ensure the proceedings are traceable, official, and properly logged.

☐ Defaulters’ Register Number present (Unit + Station)
☐ Year correctly reflected and consistent across all pages
☐ Unit / Formation / Station clearly indicated
☐ All pages belong to the same case (same defaulter name & force number)
☐ Dates flow logically (no future dates, no contradictions)
☐ No missing mandatory pages (Defaulter Sheet, Evidence, Committee Record)

**Red flags**

* Different dates that don’t reconcile
* Missing register numbers
* Pages not signed but referenced elsewhere

---

## 2. JURISDICTION & AUTHORITY CHECK

Purpose: Confirm the committee had legal power to sit.

☐ Presiding Officer named, ranked, and force number provided
☐ Presiding Officer rank is legally competent for the offence
☐ Assistant Presiding Officer appointed
☐ Prosecutor appointed
☐ Observer appointed
☐ Orderly NCO appointed
☐ Committee composition listed separately and signed
☐ Venue and time of sitting recorded

**Red flags**

* Presiding officer below required rank
* Missing observer
* Committee details only appearing once and nowhere else

---

## 3. DEFUALTER IDENTIFICATION CHECK

Purpose: Ensure there is no doubt who was tried.

☐ Force number correct and consistent everywhere
☐ Rank consistent throughout the proceedings
☐ Name in BLOCK LETTERS at first mention
☐ Unit / posting indicated
☐ Service details completed (enlistment, pay, character, previous offences)

**Red flags**

* Force number mismatch
* Different spellings of name
* Service details missing

---

## 4. OFFENCE FORMULATION CHECK (VERY IMPORTANT)

Purpose: Ensure the charge is legally sound.

☐ Offence stated in BLOCK CAPITALS
☐ Correct section cited (NPS Act + Schedule)
☐ Sub-section correctly stated
☐ Date(s) of offence clearly defined
☐ Place of offence clearly stated
☐ Duration calculated correctly (23 days here)
☐ Particulars mirror the charge exactly

**Golden rule**

> If the particulars don’t *perfectly* support the charge, the case is weak.

---

## 5. NOTIFICATION & FAIR HEARING CHECK

Purpose: Protect the proceedings from appeal.

☐ Defaulter notified in writing
☐ Legal Notice No. 90 (1)/2015 served
☐ Date of notification recorded
☐ Defaulter confirms notification on record
☐ Asked if he objects to committee members
☐ Language of proceedings recorded
☐ Defaulter confirms understanding

**Red flags**

* “Notified” ticked YES but no date
* Language not indicated

---

## 6. ASSISTANCE & DEFENCE RIGHTS CHECK

Purpose: Confirm compliance with Standing Orders.

☐ Defaulter informed of right to assistance
☐ Decision recorded (Yes / No)
☐ If No, clearly stated
☐ If Yes, name of assisting officer recorded

---

## 7. PLEA TAKING CHECK

Purpose: This is where most files collapse.

☐ Plea recorded in defaulter’s **own words**
☐ Plea language recorded
☐ Plea signed by defaulter
☐ Plea corresponds to the charge
☐ Conviction expressly stated after plea

**Red flags**

* “Plea of guilty entered” without defaulter’s words
* No signature

---

## 8. EVIDENCE CHECK

Purpose: Ensure the conviction is supported even if plea is challenged.

☐ Precise of Evidence attached
☐ Evidence narrative is chronological
☐ OB numbers cited
☐ Signals referenced and dated
☐ Efforts to trace defaulter documented
☐ Evidence supports period of absence
☐ Evidence signed by Presiding Officer

---

## 9. JUDGEMENT & CONVICTION CHECK

Purpose: Formal legality.

☐ Judgment clearly states conviction
☐ Basis of conviction stated (own plea / evidence)
☐ Judgment signed by committee members

---

## 10. MITIGATION CHECK

Purpose: Fairness and proportionality.

☐ Defaulter invited to mitigate
☐ Mitigation recorded verbatim or summarized
☐ Defaulter signed mitigation

---

## 11. SENTENCING POWERS CHECK

Purpose: Prevent ultra vires punishment.

☐ Presiding officer assesses own powers
☐ Decision on adequacy of powers recorded
☐ Recommendation clearly stated
☐ Reason for recommendation provided

---

## 12. SENTENCE & POST-TRIAL RIGHTS CHECK

Purpose: Close the procedural loop.

☐ Sentence section present (even if blank pending higher authority)
☐ Defaulter informed of right to appeal
☐ Appeal period correctly stated
☐ Defaulter acknowledges appeal rights
☐ Punishment publication section present

---

## 13. SIGNATURE & DATE INTEGRITY CHECK

Purpose: Administrative cleanliness.

☐ All required signatures present
☐ Dates next to signatures
☐ No unsigned critical sections
☐ No backdating indicators

---

## 14. INTERNAL CONSISTENCY CHECK (FINAL SWEEP)

Purpose: Catch silent killers.

☐ Dates of offence consistent everywhere
☐ Number of days consistent everywhere
☐ Sections of law consistent everywhere
☐ Findings in summary match main proceedings
